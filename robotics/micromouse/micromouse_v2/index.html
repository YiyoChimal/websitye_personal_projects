Micromouse V1 (2024)

Objective

Design a second robot, improving the speed of processing, physical speed, an the manufacturing than in our first disign. Aim to compete in the second robot competition carried out in my university (Anahuac Veracruz) in the category Micromouse.

My Role

• Electronics hardware designer (search the components, make the electronics schematics and do the PCB).

• Firmware programmer (sensors and actuators integration, algorithm and filters implementation).

• Test and implementation.

Thecnical Specifications

Specification Details
Name V1
Width 100mm
Length 120mm
Weight 187 g
Wheel 3D printed rim + Rubber wheel D22mm
Processor ATXMEGA64A3U-AU
Memory MCU built- 64 KB Flash memory, 4 KB of internal SRAM, and 2048 bytes (2 KB) of EEPROM
Motor/Encoder GA12 N20 DC 6V
Gear ratio 20:1 / wheel D:22mm
Battery 1800mAh 3.7v 4.44wh x 2
Internal Power Regulation AMS1117 regulator 5V 1A SOT-223v & 3.3V 1A SOT-223
Motor Power Supply MT3608 to 7V, 2A Max
Motor Driver L293d
IR Sensor sfh4545 & teft4300
MEMS MPU-9250
User Interface Mudule HC-06 Bluetooth, LEDs x 2, Button, latching push button x 2
Max straight speed 0.806 m/s
max search speed 0.5642 m/s
diagonal run turn(max) 0.403 m/s
acceleration/deceleration 34.0 m/s2
